About us

             OYSTER KODE is coding community established in Rajarambapu Institute of Technology on13 Jan 2020 in presence of club mentor Prof. A. B. Patil and discipline committee head Prof. S. Y. Chougule. Started with 6 core members club further expanded to 30+ members. In initial days club was working on basic programing languages and basic technologies in computer science engineering.

              Oyster Kode recruited 20 members after three months of establishment. Expansion of club resulted in introduction of different new technologies in club members. Further we started working on different projects related to web technology, mobile application development and cyber security. Computer Science and Engineering department issued workspace for club activities, we specially thank to department HOD Dr. N. V. Dharwadkar and faculty.

              Phase 0.1 was led by Mr. Avinash Pawar and Mr. Musheer Jamadar, phase ended with completion of 3 projects, one in Android Development and other two in Web technology. Phase 0.1 was planned to divide club members in two groups ‘Web Technology’ & ‘Cyber Security’, web was led by Mr. Rohit Kalubarme and Cyber was led by Mr. Musheer Jamadar. And at that time core members were working on ‘Mobile Application Development’. Computer Science and Information Technology department issued workspace for club activities, we specially thank to club mentor prof. A. B. Patil and former HOD Dr. A. C. Adamuthe.

FOUNDER

OYSTER KODE CLUB RIT

                   I am indeed happy to note your interest in our OYSTER KODE CLUB. On this web site we have presented a brief profile of our club. I would very much like all of you to know about our club. We established OYSTER KODE in 2020 with a realization that all-round social transformation can be brought about through the spread of passive education and for that we believe in creating platforms for better growth as early conceptual understanding is essential for students to be in a position to re-skill and up-skill themselves at any given point in the coming times.

              The challenge since the beginning has been around changing the perception or mindset about how the quality and reliable resource can be provided to students in the remotest part of the country. Our vision is to give access to quality resources for those who are from rural areas and having lack of resources. Going forward , our approach is to further accelerate our reach and provide platform for students from different lnstitutes and Universities and try to touch new highs of technology.
