Events

Oyster kode has been always trying of creating platforms for students from RIT and different institutions to express their skills. Events organized by club includes technical workshops, technical & non-technical competition. We believe these events help students to enhance their competitive skills and performance. These events give leadership, problem solving, communication, management like opportunities to club members.
